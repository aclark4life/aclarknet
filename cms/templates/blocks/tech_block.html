Pillow

Pillow is the most-downloaded Python imaging library. A four-person developer team keeps it alive.

In 2010 Alex Clark created Pillow, a software development fork of the Python Imaging Library. As of early 2017, Pillow has been downloaded over 15 million times and is used by Fortune-100-serving companies like Atlassian. Support the team directly via GitHub Sponsors.
